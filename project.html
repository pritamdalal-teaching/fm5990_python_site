If you are going to choose your own project, please send me an e-mail letting me know what it is.


Data Set

This data set consists of the PNLs for selling weekly 30-delta strangles on various underlyings over a period of 5 years. Each individual is responsible for answering the questions below for one underlying (which I will assign to you).

Project Data


Questions to Answer

  1. All the underlyings are ETFs. Write a 1-2 sentence summary of the investment strategy of your underlying.

  2. Identify the ten worst months in terms of PNL (group by month/year of expiration date). Determine if losses were for idiosyncratic reasons or systemic reasons. Explain your classification for each of a the 10 months with a 1-2 sentence explanation.

  3. Check for large discrepancies between implied vols and the volatility index. How many of these large discrepancies occured during periods of high market turbulence?

  4. Bonus: Demonstrate the presence of the leverage effect in your underlying by regressing returns against changes in the volatility index.
